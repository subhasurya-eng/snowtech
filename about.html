Academy Overview

Who We Are

Snow Tech is a creative learning academy founded to bridge the gap between traditional education and the rapidly evolving digital creative industry. We believe every learner deserves world-class guidance — regardless of background.

Since our founding, we have trained thousands of students across Tamil Nadu, helping them launch careers in digital art, animation, UI/UX, web development, and more.

Our classrooms are vibrant, our instructors are industry veterans, and our curriculum is updated every semester to reflect real industry demands.
